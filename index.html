My Hobbies



My favorite Hobby is drawing

Drawing is my favorite hobby. I cannot imagine a colorful life without drawing.It is my believe that to display your art you do not have to be a great artist.You just have to make a unique piece. So get your paint brush and pencil and start drawing. Drawing is a way for me to show my creativity. However drawing is not as easy as it seems to be. It needs a lot of devotion as well as practice. I can never feel bored when I have my colors with me. My colors are my best friend.
